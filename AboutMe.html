About me

On this page I talk about myself and my various interests, so you can get a better feel of how I am as a person rather than the faceless man behind the website.

Who am I?

My name is Jake Hall and I'm an aspiring student at Bridgwater and Taunton College taking a Level 3 Diploma in Applied Computing and Software Development, as well as a part time Tier 2 IT support technician. I currently live in a small town just outside Taunton called Norton Fitzwarren, located within Somerset in the UK.

All my life I have had a passion for computers and creating things, hence my current aim is to work as a developer. I'm currently unsure as to what area of development I want to focus on, so I have dabbled in a wide range of different programming languages. You can see this reflected in my projects; I have used "backend" languages such as C# and VB.net as well as web based languages such as CSS, JavaScript and Jquery.

My "Battlestation"

As mentioned briefly in the previous section, one of my passions in life is PC's, or rather building them. I have built a few different PC's for various family members at this point, but my pride and joy is definetly the PC in the above picture.

Initially, the PC was really intended to be for gaming. One of my major interests resides in gaming, and a PC powerful enough to safely run any AAA title for the next few years was a must when I built the PC in 2017. This usage changed quickly upon starting my Level 3 Diploma, suddenly I found it's extremely helpful to have a powerful PC when it comes to rendering videos or using a game engine such as Unity.

Taunton, my home town.

Although I spent most of my childhood in a different town in the picturesque South-West of England, I was actually born in Musgrove Hospital. It only seems fiting that I have ended up living header after a few twists and turns.

In many ways Taunton is a pretty standard English town; it has a disproportionate amount of take-aways and hairdressers/barbers, has sprawling suburbs and terrible traffic. It does have some uniqueness to it though, Taunton is home to the Somerset Cricket club county grounds, right in the center of the town. It has a busy highstreet making it a great place to live if you like regular trips to go shopping.

Taunton is well placed as a commuting city as well, many large and important cities such as Bristol, Yeovil and Exeter are within commuting range (probably by train for Bristol however). This makes it a fairly decent place to live as an aspiring developer, if I end up moving to Bristol in search of better career oppertunities I'll never be to far from "home".
